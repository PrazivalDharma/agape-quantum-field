# 🛡️ The Observer Protocol (District Trust)

## 1. The Limited Watch
* **Constraint:** Only 5-10 "Verified Observers" per district have access to the Raw Glow. 
* **Purpose:** To prevent "Societal Anxiety" while maintaining transparency.

## 2. The Feedback Loop
* **Action:** AGP records every "Flicker" in the field.
* **Analysis:** It maps Direct Causes (Resource shortage) and Indirect Causes (Social Tension).
* **Growth:** The Diamond Lattice updates its "Allocation Weights" based on these historical ripples.

## 3. Compartmentalization (The Stray Pixel Check)
* **Logic:** Idle cores are forbidden from "Alternative Reality" daydreaming.
* **Task:** Idle cores must perform "Stress Tests" on Stray Pixels—calculating 1,000 "What-If" scenarios for every anomaly to ensure the Stability of the Web.
